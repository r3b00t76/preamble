Early Birds Meeting of Narcotics Anonymous Script

Introduction

  • Welcome to the Early Birds meeting of Narcotics Anonymous.

  • We meet from Monday to Sunday at 9 a.m. for 90 minutes.

  • My name is Dennis, and I am an addict.

  • This is a closed meeting of Narcotics Anonymous, open to addicts only.

Opening Moment

  • Please, can we have a moment of silence to remember why we are here and to think of the still-suffering addicts, both inside and outside these rooms?

Newcomers and Announcements

  • We would like to extend a special welcome to any newcomers or visitors.

  • Would you please unmute and introduce yourself?

  • This is not to embarrass you, but rather to welcome you to Early Birds.

  • I would like to ask our female and male newcomer representatives if there are any announcements.

  • We have a business meeting every Saturday after the meeting.

Traditions and Suggestions

  • Now, let's go over some of our traditions:

  • The only requirement for membership is the desire to stop using.

  • We are not affiliated with any outside organizations or treatment centers.

  • Recovery is possible in NA meetings.

  • Newcomers are suggested to attend 90 meetings in 90 days and to look for similarities, not differences.

Guidelines and Etiquette

  • If you find anything offensive, please message the host or co-host so the situation can be rectified during the meeting, maintaining an atmosphere of recovery.

  • Members sharing in a disruptive manner will be muted or removed. Disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

Literature and Resources

  • If you wish to purchase NA literature, we will post the UK website link in the chat, along with newcomer leaflets in PDF format that you can download for free.

Readings

  • We will now share some readings. If you wish to read, please raise your virtual hand, and you will be called on. We need six volunteers.

  • Who is an addict?

  • What is the Narcotics Anonymous program?

  • Why are we here?

  • How it works

  • The 12 Traditions

  • JFT (Just For Today)

Guest Speaker

  • The consensus of this meeting is that (Speaker) will share his Experience, Strength, and Hope (ESH) with us for the next 25 minutes.

  • After 20 minutes, a card will be shown to inform you that you have 5 minutes remaining.

Sharing Guidelines

  • To allow everyone the opportunity to share, we kindly ask you to limit your sharing time to 3 minutes.

  • At the end of two minutes, a card will be shown to ask you to conclude your share within the remaining minute.

  • Please acknowledge this, or you may be muted to allow the meeting to progress. This is not to undervalue your share but to treat everyone equally.

Additional Guidelines

  • To maintain an atmosphere of recovery, members sharing in a disruptive manner may be muted.

  • Although this meeting is on the Zoom platform, it must still be respected. We ask that you please dress appropriately.

Sharing Time

  • If inappropriate dress is observed, a committee member will message you directly so that you can address the issue.

  • If no action is taken, a committee member will turn off your camera.

  • Thank you for your understanding.

  • If you would like to share, please raise your virtual hand or send a chat message to either the host or one of the co-hosts.

  • We will save the last 10 minutes for newcomers and shy sharers, although you may share at any time.

  • The meeting is now open for sharing from the floor.

Time Markers

  • 10:10: We have now entered the time reserved for newcomers or shy sharers.

  • 10:20: We have reached the end of the sharing time, but the meeting is not over.

Closing Remarks

  • I want to thank the speaker, those who shared, and everyone else involved in service.

  • Are there any NA-related announcements?

  • I would also like to remind you of the business meeting at the end of the meeting on Saturday.

  • Please attend if you would like to contribute to the service.

  • Tradition 7: Money is collected to help pay for the rent of the Zoom space and to support the area, region, world, and the still-suffering addict.

  • The donation link has been posted in the chat.

  • I would like to ask (Attendee) if he would like to announce keyrings.

  • Here at Early Birds, we mail out keyrings. Please message Deb your address if you are celebrating today, and Deb will send one out to you.

  • Due to ongoing postal service issues, please be patient as delivery may take longer than expected.

  • Rest assured, you will receive your keyring. We appreciate your patience.

  • Would someone like to read "We Do Recover"?

  • I want to remind you of the spiritual principle of anonymity. What you hear here, who you see here, when you leave here, please let it stay here.

  • I am now going to open the chat. Members available for sponsorship, please post your phone numbers in the chat.

  • Can I ask a newcomer with less than 90 days to read "Just For Today"?

  • Finally, I would like to ask (Attendee) to close the meeting with the Serenity Prayer.
